merlion

The Merlion is the national personification of Singapore

It's name combines "mer", meaning the sea and "lion". The fish body represents Singapores origin as a fishing village when it was called Temasek, which means "sea town" in Javanese. The lion head represents Singapore's original name Singapura meaning "lion city" or "kota singa".

10 bayfron Avenue, Singapore
